About me

One and half years of research experience in Federated Learning projects with AIRS, CUHKSZ, Yale, IBM Research, and WeBank, including optimization, system prototype development, multi-robot collaborative learning, and incentive mechanism design. Four years of working experience in China Mobile Headquarters as a project manager, leading several projects with Huawei, ZTE, Ericsson, and Nokia in improving the quality of China Mobile wireless network. Three years completing a Ph.D. degree from Melbourne University, earning three prestigious scholarships.

Research Interest

  • Federated learning

  • Reinforcement learning

  • Robotics learning

  • Optimization in wireless networks, communications and energy harvesting

  • Cooperative game theory
